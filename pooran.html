about the fock culture

Rajasthan is famous for textiles, semi-precious stones and handicrafts as well as for its traditional and colorful commonly ballads art.
Rajasthani furniture is known for its intricate carvings and bright colours.
Block prints, tie and dye prints, Bagaru prints, Sanganer prints and Zari embroidery are famous.

Precious And Semi PreciousStones Rajasthan has rich deposits of precious and semi-precious stones such as emerald, garnet, agate, amethyst, topaz and lapiz lazuli.
Each gem is believed to have inherent powers and are used as lucky charms along with their beauty as jewellery pieces.
Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, India, is known as the "Gemstone Capital of the World."
For centuries, this city has been a hub for precious and semi-precious stones, attracting buyers and sellers from all around the globe.
